# Content Engine Automation System

## What It Does

Transforms a single content idea into a full week of platform-ready posts — automatically. Feed it a topic, target audience, and tone, and it outputs hooks, scripts, captions, and hashtags for Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more.

## Workflow Flow

```
Content Brief Input (Airtable / Google Sheet / Form)
        ↓
   AI Hook Generation (10 options)
        ↓
   Hook Selected (manual or auto top-pick)
        ↓
   AI Script / Caption Generation
        ↓
   Platform Formatting (IG Reel, LinkedIn, TikTok, Twitter/X)
        ↓
   Content Calendar Entry Created
        ↓
   Scheduled to Buffer / Later / GHL Social Planner
```

## Outcomes

- 📅 **Full content calendar** generated in minutes, not hours
- 🎯 **Consistent messaging** — same idea, adapted per platform
- 🤖 **AI-powered hooks** that match your audience's language
- ♻️ **Repurposing built in** — one idea → 5+ pieces of content

## Ideal For

- Agency owners managing social for multiple clients
- Coaches building personal brand content
- Business owners who need consistent content without a content team
- Marketers running multi-platform campaigns

Used to produce 20–30 pieces of content per week across client accounts with one person managing it. Content quality and consistency improved significantly vs manual creation.
