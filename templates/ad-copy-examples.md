# Ad Copy Templates

Proven frameworks for Meta, Google, and LinkedIn ads. Fill in the brackets and adapt to your offer.

---

## Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads

### Template 1 — Problem/Solution
```
HEADLINE: [Audience] — stop [doing painful thing]

BODY:
If you're [doing X] and not seeing [result], here's why:

[Common mistake or misconception]

The good news? There's a simpler way.

[What your solution does differently]

[Social proof — X clients, X results]

[CTA] → [Landing page]
```

**Example:**
```
HEADLINE: Coaches — stop chasing leads manually

BODY:
If you're DMing 50 people a day and booking 2 calls, here's why:

You're doing the work that automation should handle.

The good news? There's a simpler way.

Our system captures, qualifies, and books leads automatically — while you focus on delivery.

Used by 40+ coaches to book 15–30 calls/month on autopilot.

👉 See how it works → [link]
```

---

### Template 2 — Social Proof Lead
```
HEADLINE: How [Client type] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]

BODY:
[Client name/type] came to us [time] ago.

They were struggling with [problem].

Within [timeframe], they [specific result].

Here's exactly what we did: [brief explanation]

If you're in [similar situation], this might be for you.

[CTA]
```

---

### Template 3 — Curiosity/Contrarian
```
HEADLINE: Why [common belief] is keeping [audience] stuck

BODY:
Most [audience] think [common belief].

But here's the truth: [contrarian insight]

We've worked with [X] businesses in [niche] and the ones getting results are doing the opposite.

[Brief insight or framework]

Curious? [CTA]
```

## Google Ads

### Search Ad Template
```
HEADLINE 1: [Main keyword + benefit] (30 chars)
HEADLINE 2: [Social proof or differentiator] (30 chars)
HEADLINE 3: [CTA] (30 chars)

DESCRIPTION 1: [Problem + solution in 90 chars]
DESCRIPTION 2: [Social proof + CTA in 90 chars]
```

**Example:**
```
HEADLINE 1: AI Lead Automation for Agencies
HEADLINE 2: 40+ Clients | 15–30 Calls/Month
HEADLINE 3: See How It Works — Free Demo

DESCRIPTION 1: Stop chasing leads manually. Our system captures, qualifies and books calls on autopilot.
DESCRIPTION 2: Trusted by agencies & coaches. Setup in 30 minutes. Book a free strategy call today.
```

## LinkedIn Ads

### Thought Leadership Ad
```
HOOK: [Insight or stat that stops scrolling]

BODY: [2-3 sentences expanding on the insight]

[What you/your company does]

[CTA — soft, value-focused]
```

### Lead Gen Ad
```
TARGETING: [Job title] at [Company size] in [Industry]

HEADLINE: [Specific result for their role]

BODY: [Pain point] → [Your solution] → [Outcome]

CTA BUTTON: Download / Learn More / Get Quote
```

## Retargeting Ad Copy

```
HEADLINE: Still thinking about [offer]?

BODY:
You visited [page/site] recently — just wanted to follow up.

[Reinforce key benefit or address likely objection]

[Limited-time offer or urgency if applicable]

[CTA]
```

```
The next step is [CTA action].

[Benefit + social proof]

[CTA]
```
