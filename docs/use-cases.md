# Use Cases

Real-world applications of these automation systems across different business types.

---

## E-Commerce Brands

### Abandoned Lead Recovery
Use the Lead Generation System to re-engage people who clicked an ad but didn't purchase.
- Trigger: Ad click + no purchase within 24h
- Action: WhatsApp sequence with product benefit + social proof + limited offer
- Result: Recover 10–25% of otherwise-lost leads

### Post-Purchase Upsell Automation
After a purchase, automatically offer complementary products via WhatsApp.
- Trigger: Purchase confirmation webhook
- Action: 3-day sequence with upsell offer
- Typical uplift: 15–30% attach rate on upsell

### Flash Sale Campaigns
Use the Content Engine to generate ad copy, email, and social posts for a sale — in minutes instead of hours.

---

## Coaches & Consultants

### DM → Call Funnel Automation
Someone engages with your content → they DM you → automated qualification → booking link sent if qualified.
- Use: Appointment Booking System
- Result: More booked calls without manually screening every DM

### Webinar Follow-Up System
After a webinar, automatically follow up with attendees and no-shows with different sequences.
- Attendees: "Here's the replay + next step"
- No-shows: "You missed it — here's what we covered + catch-up offer"

### Content → Leads Pipeline
Use the Content Engine to post consistently, then connect it to the Lead Gen System to capture and follow up with people who engage.

## Local Businesses

### GMB Lead Automation
Someone calls or messages via Google My Business → automatically added to CRM → follow-up sequence started.
- Use cases: Gyms, clinics, dental, real estate, trades

### WhatsApp Booking System
Replace manual appointment scheduling with automated booking.
- Customer texts "book" → receives available times → confirms → gets reminder
- Works for salons, clinics, consultants, restaurants

### Review Generation Automation
After service delivery, automatically send a WhatsApp asking for a Google review.
- Timing: 24–48 hours after appointment
- Include direct Google review link
- Typical result: 3–5x more reviews with zero manual effort

## Agencies

### Client Lead Gen Delivery
Run the Lead Generation System for clients — white-labelled and repeatable across accounts.
- Deploy once, replicate across 5–20 clients
- Reduces per-client setup from days → hours

### Reporting Automation
Use the Content Engine concept to auto-generate monthly performance reports from data.
- Pull data from client ad accounts
- AI generates narrative summary
- Deliver to client automatically

### New Client Onboarding
Automate the onboarding sequence for new clients — welcome, next steps, document requests, kick-off call booking.

## SaaS & Tech Products

### Trial-to-Paid Conversion
Identify trial users who haven't activated key features → automated WhatsApp/email sequence to help them get value.

### Demo Booking System
Qualify inbound demo requests with AI before routing to sales → higher-quality demos, fewer wasted calls.
- Use: Appointment Booking System
- Result: Sales team only talks to qualified prospects

### Churn Prevention
Identify at-risk users (low usage, support tickets) → trigger personalised outreach sequence automatically.
